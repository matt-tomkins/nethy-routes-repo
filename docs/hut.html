Meall a’ Bhuachaille

A popular Corbett standing directly above the Youth Hostel. Here is a chance to experience the beauty and wildness of the Cairngorms without the length and remoteness of some of the Munro walks

  • Difficulty: Easy / Medium
  • Length: 8.5 km
  • Ascent: 543 m
  • Start: Glenmore Lodge
  • Description: Glenmore Lodge → Ryvoan Pass → west to Meall a’ Bhuachaille summit → west to bealach → descent via Coire Chondlaich
  • GPX: Download

Meall a’ Bhuachaille to Craiggowrie

As above, but continuing along the undulating ridge to Craiggowrie. Great views to the high Cairngorms and west to the Monadhliath

  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Length: 16.3 km
  • Ascent: 768 m
  • Start: Glenmore Lodge
  • Description: Glenmore Lodge → Ryvoan Pass → west to Meall a’ Bhuachaille summit → west to bealach → Creagan Gorm → Craiggowrie → descent south-west to forest edge → forest tracks to Badaguish Outdoor Centre
  • GPX: Download

Bynack More

Bynack More is a prominent Munro, the northeastern cornerstone of the Cairngorms. It lacks any grand corries but is a magnificent viewpoint for northeast Scotland.

  • Difficulty: Medium / Hard
  • Length: 22 km
  • Ascent: 860 m
  • Start: Glenmore Lodge
  • Description: Glenmore Lodge → Ryvoan Pass → River Nethy → Bynack More → Return by the outbound route or alternatively via Bynack Beg
  • GPX: Download linear route or via Bynack Beg

The Chalamain Gap

A lower level walk to pass through the bouldery defile of the Chalamain Gap. Be cautious in winter conditions: a known avalanche hotspot

  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Length: 17 km
  • Ascent: 454 m
  • Start: Glenmore Lodge
  • Description: Glenmore Lodge → forest tracks to the Sugar Bowl car park → west to the Chalamain Gap → descent to Lairig Ghru path → north along the banks of the Allt Druidh → Rothiemurchus Lodge → Loch Morlich → Glenmore
  • GPX: Download

Loch Morlich

An easy flat walk on forest tracks around Loch Morlich, with the stunning backdrop of the often snow-clad peaks of the northern Cairngorms. Lots of possibilities to modify and extend the route - the shortest variant is ~6 km

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Length: 6 km
  • Ascent: 26 m
  • Start: Glenmore Lodge
  • Description: Follow the signs!
  • GPX: Download

Loch an Eilein

An easy undulating walk on forest tracks to reach the beautiful Loch an Eilein. If transport is available, the circuit of the loch is ~7 km (parking charge applies)

  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Length: 23.5 km
  • Ascent: 235 m
  • Start: Glenmore Lodge
  • Description: Many possible variations, one of which is: Glenmore Lodge → south side of Loch Morlich → Cairngorm Club footbridge → Lochan Deo → Loch an Eilein → Rothiemurchus → return via the Old Logging Way
  • GPX: Download
